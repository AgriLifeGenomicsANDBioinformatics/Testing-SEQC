Description:
  Evaluates de quality of an assembly with a reference.

Usage:
	refEvalPE.sh  [OPTIONS]  -- ASSEMBLY.fasta REFERENCE.fasta READ_1.[fq|fastq] READ_2.[fq|fastq]  OUTDIR

Options:
  -h--help                Help.
  -t--threads             Threads used by DETONATE. Default: 1.

Examples:
  refEvalPE.sh -- assemblies/toy_assembly_1.fa reference/toy_ref.fa reads/toy_SE.fq reads/toy_SE.fq refEvalPEOut

Upstream:
  trinityPE.sh

Dependancies:
  blat
  ref-eval
  rsem
  bowtie
