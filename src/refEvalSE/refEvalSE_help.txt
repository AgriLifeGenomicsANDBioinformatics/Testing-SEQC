Description:
  Evaluates de quality of an assembly with a reference and single ended reads.

Usage:
	refEvaSPE.sh  [OPTIONS]  -- ASSEMBLY.fasta REFERENCE.fasta READ_1.fastq.gz  OUTDIR

Options:
  -h--help                Help.
  -t--threads             Threads used by DETONATE. Default: 1.
  -k--kmerSize            Kmer size used by ref-eval. Default: 25.

Examples:
  refEvalSE.sh -t 16 -- assemblies/toy_assembly_1.fa reference/toy_ref.fa reads/toy_SE.fq refEvalPEOut

Upstream:
  trinityPE.sh

Dependancies:
  blat
  ref-eval
  rsem-calculate-expression
  rsem-prepare-reference
  bowtie-build
