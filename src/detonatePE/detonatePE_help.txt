Description:
  Wrapper for DETONATE with paired end reads.

Usage:
	detonatePE.sh  [OPTIONS]  -- read_r1.fastq.gz read_r2.fastq.gz  assembly.fasta

Options:
  -h--help                Help.
  -t--threads             Threads used by DETONATE. Default: 1.

Examples:
  detonatePE.sh -e -t 2 -- reads/reads_r1.fastq.gz reads/reads_r2.fastq.gz assembly/trinityOut.fasta
