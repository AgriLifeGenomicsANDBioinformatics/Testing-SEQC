Description:
  Wrapper for SEECER with paired end reads.

Usage:
	seecerPE.sh  [OPTIONS]  -- read_r1.fastq.gz read_r2.fastq.gz

Options:
  -h--help                Help.
  -d--outdir              Output directory. Default: seecerOut.
  -k--kmerSize            Kmer size. Default: 24.

Examples:
  seecerPE.sh -d seecer20 -k 20 -- read_r1.fastq.gz read_r2.fastq.gz

Upstream:
  filteringPE.sh
